Our Vision and Mission statement

  • To make heaven.
  • To take as many people with us.
  • To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
  • To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  • To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  • We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.

Pastor's Charge

Welcome in the name of Jesus Christ, our Savior and King! I charge you today to rise to the call of God upon your life. You were not born by accident, nor are you here by chance. You are God’s masterpiece, created for a divine purpose, and this is your moment to walk boldly in it. In a world filled with noise, fear, and uncertainty, I call you to be a light—rooted in truth, burning with love, and empowered by the Holy Spirit. Let your faith not be passive, but alive and active. Stand firm in righteousness, pursue peace, extend grace, and never forget the hope we have in Christ. Join us, not just as a spectator, but as a vessel ready to be filled and poured out for God’s glory. There is work to be done, lives to be changed, and a Kingdom to advance. Be strong. Be courageous. Be faithful. The time is now. Welcome home.

Fortune Ayodele Pastor
